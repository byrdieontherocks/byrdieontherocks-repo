Byrdie on the Rocks

Hey, I’m Byrd – a tech marketer and an amateur climber who’s been exploring the climbing scene in Thailand for almost three years. I started lead climbing about a year ago, and it’s been a mix of fun, challenge, and discovery. This blog is where I share my personal climbing experiences – the good, the messy, and everything in between. Just a heads-up: I’m not a professional climber, and this isn’t a place for expert advice. Please make sure to do your own research and stay safe out there!

I’m also using this space to practice my web development skills, so you’ll probably notice the site evolving here and there. It’s all part of the process. Thanks for stopping by! Feel free to look around and join me on this little adventure.
