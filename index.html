This is ByrdieOnTheRocks Climbing Blogs!

Hello fellow climbers! I’m Byrdie, also known as ByrdieOnTheRocks. I’m an amateur climber, a professional marketer, and a rookie web developer based in beautiful Thailand. Welcome to my little corner of the internet where I share my climbing adventures across Thailand and explore the amazing climbing spots all over Asia.
This isn’t a typical knowledge-sharing site. I’m still very much on my climbing journey, constantly learning and improving with every ascent. Alongside my climbing passion, I’m also diving into web development, using this space to practice my skills and grow. So, don’t be surprised if things change a little as I try out new ideas and experiment with my website.
Feel free to explore my journey, celebrate the highs, face the challenges, and discover Asia’s top climbing spots. Whether you're into climbing or web development, I hope you enjoy my content!
